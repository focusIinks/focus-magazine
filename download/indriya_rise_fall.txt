EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
─────────────────
Indriya X Research Centre (branded as IndriyaX) is a ~6-month-old, founder-led,
bootstrapped optometry education platform based in India. Founded by Anik Dingal,
a practicing optometrist and research scholar, the platform delivers webinars,
clinical education series (OptoTalks), and community networking for optometry
students and professionals — with a niche focus on neuro-optometry.

The trajectory is: VERY EARLY RISE with severe structural headwinds. The entity
has demonstrated genuine execution velocity (10+ webinars, 6+ partnerships,
content series launch) but operates with zero infrastructure — no website, no
revenue model, no accreditation, and a brand name that is invisible in search
due to collision with a ₹5,000 crore Aditya Birla jewellery brand.

This is not a "rise and fall" story — it's a "rise and plateau" story with a
non-trivial risk of "plateau becoming decline" within 12-18 months.
