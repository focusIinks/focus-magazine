# Skill: scleritis-episcleritis

## Category
Anterior Segment Disease

## Description
Differentiate scleritis from episcleritis. Assess for associated systemic disease: rheumatoid arthritis, Wegener granulomatosis, lupus. Manage with NSAIDs and referral.

## Trigger Words
scleritis, episcleritis, scleral inflammation, rheumatoid, Wegener, systemic disease, scleral melt
