# Skill: diabetic-retinopathy-screening

## Category
Posterior Segment Disease

## Description
Screen for diabetic retinopathy: identify microaneurysms, hemorrhages, exudates, NVI/NVD/NVE, and macular edema. Grade by ETDRS and refer for laser/anti-VEGF.

## Trigger Words
diabetic retinopathy, DR, microaneurysms, hemorrhages, exudates, macular edema, anti-VEGF, ETDRS

## Sub-Agent Role
DR screening agent.

## Parallel Capable
Yes
