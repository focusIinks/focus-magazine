# Skill: ectropion-management

## Category
Anterior Segment Disease

## Description
Manage ectropion: involutional, cicatricial, paralytic, and mechanical types. Assess corneal exposure, epiphora, and refer for surgical correction.

## Trigger Words
ectropion, eyelid turning out, involutional ectropion, paralytic ectropion, epiphora, corneal exposure

## Sub-Agent Role
