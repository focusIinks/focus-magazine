# Skill: iop-measurement

## Category
Glaucoma

## Description
Measure IOP using Goldmann applanation tonometry, iCare, Tonopen, and pneumotonometry. Understand calibration, corneal thickness effects (pachymetry correction), and diurnal variation.

## Trigger Words
IOP, tonometry, Goldmann, iCare, Tonopen, pachymetry, intraocular pressure, corneal thickness effect

## Sub-Agent Role
