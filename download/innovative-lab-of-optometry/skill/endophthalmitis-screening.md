# Skill: endophthalmitis-screening

## Category
Anterior Segment Disease

## Description
Recognize endophthalmitis signs: hypopyon, severe vitritis, reduced VA, pain post-surgery/post-trauma. Emergency referral for vitreous tap and injection.

## Trigger Words
endophthalmitis, hypopyon, post-surgical infection, vitreous tap, intraocular infection, severe eye pain

## Sub-Agent Role
Endophthalmitis emergency screener.
