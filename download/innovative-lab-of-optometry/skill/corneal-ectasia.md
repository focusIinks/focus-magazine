# Skill: corneal-ectasia

## Category
Anterior Segment Disease

## Description
Diagnose corneal ectatic disorders: keratoconus, pellucid marginal degeneration, keratoglobus. Use topography, pachymetry, and Belin/Ambrosio indices.

## Trigger Words
keratoconus, ectasia, pellucid, keratoglobus, corneal thinning, topography, Belin Ambrosio, ectatic

## Sub-Agent Role
Corneal ectasia diagnostician.

## Parallel Capable
