### Is "OptoBharat" a Rebrand?

**Yes, partially.** The company operates under the umbrella brand "Envision Bharat" (envisionbharat.com) while "OptoBharat" (optobharat.com) appears to be the dedicated domain for the OptoAI education product. The naming is confusing:
- "Envision Bharat" = the company
- "OptoBharat" = the OptoAI product's domain (not the company name)
- "OptoAI" = the product name
- "optobharat.com" = the product URL

**The domain name "optobharat.com" suggests the company may have originally planned to call itself "OptoBharat" before settling on "Envision Bharat" as the parent company name.** The fact that envisionbharat.com was registered first (archived August 2024) while optobharat.com has never been archived suggests optobharat.com was registered later and possibly went through a non-public development phase before its current deployment.
