About Focus

The essential reading for optometrists who demand clinical depth, practice innovation, and career growth.

Our Mission

Focus Magazine was founded with a singular purpose: to elevate the standard of optometric education by delivering clinically rigorous, peer-reviewed content that optometrists can immediately apply in practice.

We bridge the gap between academic research and day-to-day clinical practice. Every article is reviewed by practicing optometrists and ophthalmologists to ensure accuracy, relevance, and practical utility.

"In an era of information overload, Focus Magazine curates what matters most — the knowledge that directly improves patient outcomes."

What Sets Us Apart

Unlike generic medical publications, Focus Magazine is built exclusively for the optometric profession. Our content spans the full scope of optometric practice — from refraction fundamentals to cutting-edge myopia control, from pediatric vision screening to neuro-ophthalmic emergencies.

Each article is structured for the busy clinician: clear clinical takeaways, evidence levels cited, and practical implementation tips. We respect your time by ensuring every minute spent reading delivers real value.

Published by Focuslinks, Focus Magazine serves as the knowledge hub for the innovative lab of optometry — connecting 47,000+ optometrists across 40+ countries with the latest advancements in eye care.
