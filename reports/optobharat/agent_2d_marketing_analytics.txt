  any WhatsApp group of optometry students? They mention webinars,
  networking, and eye camps — but so does every other student community.
  There is NO unique value proposition (UVP).
- The brand positioning is aspirational, not actual. They say "India's
  leading optometry student community" but operate on a free SimDif
  website builder. The gap between claimed position and actual
  infrastructure is catastrophic for credibility.
- No brand guidelines document exists. No brand book, no style guide, no
  tone-of-voice document. The brand is whatever the last Instagram post
  said.

ASSESSMENT: The brand NAME is a 7/10. The brand POSITIONING is a 2/10.
Average: 4/10. You can't claim to be "India's Largest" from a free
website builder. The positioning-implementation gap is a credibility
