Collaborate with OPTOBHARAT

 

 
 

We believe real impact in eye care comes through strong collaboration. OPTOBHARAT partners with organizations, institutions, and industry leaders to build an accessible, innovative, and nationwide optometry ecosystem.

For Speakers & Experts

(Optometrists, ophthalmologists, educators, industry leaders)

Deliver guest lectures, webinars, and masterclasses.

Share clinical knowledge, research insights, and career guidance.

Mentor students and young professionals across our network.

Contribute to workshops, conferences, and awareness programs.

Join us in shaping the next generation of optometry professionals.

👉 Apply as a 
Speaker
 / 
Mentor

For Clinics & Eye Hospitals

Offer internships, training, and clinical exposure to students.

Conduct community eye screening & outreach programs.

Collaborate on awareness campaigns and vision care initiatives.

For Organizations & NGOs

(Schools, NGOs, community groups, foundations)

Collaborate on public health and vision awareness initiatives.

Organize eye screening camps in communities and institutions.

Support school eye health and preventive care programs.

Partner for social impact projects in underserved areas.

For Industry & Companies

(Optical brands, pharma, startups, healthcare companies)

Sponsor events, webinars, and national campaigns.

Launch and promote products through our optometry network.

Collaborate on research, innovation, and awareness programs.

For Corporates (CSR Partnerships)

Organize employee vision screening and wellness programs.

Support rural and underserved eye care initiatives.

Fund high-impact projects under CSR initiatives.

 For Universities & Institutes

(Optometry schools, colleges, training institutes)

Start OPTOBHARAT campus chapters.

Host workshops, seminars, and skill development programs.

Provide students with national-level exposure and networking.

Invite Us for Eye Screening Camps

Schools, NGOs, offices, and community organizations can collaborate with us to organize:

Free or low-cost eye check-up camps.

Vision awareness and preventive care programs.

School and workplace eye health initiatives.

We manage planning, coordination, and execution with our trained team.
